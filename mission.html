Our Mission & Process

How we are revolutionizing manufacturing

01

Start & Feed

The Omron PLC checks Sensor 1 for material. If present, it signals the Arduino Nano to drive the stepper motor, feeding material until Sensor 2 confirms it's in position.

02

Clamp & Machine

The PLC engages the Pneumatic Chuck (3 single-acting cylinders) to secure the part. A 10-second machining cycle is simulated.

03

Robot Transfer

The Fanuc 200iD Robot receives a signal to grab the finished part and transfer it to the inspection station.

04

3D Scan & Sort

A Raspberry Pi controls a line laser and camera to scan the rotating part. It compares the geometry to the CAD file and tells the PLC to sort the part as Good, Scrap, or Rework.
